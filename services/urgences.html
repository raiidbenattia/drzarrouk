What is Teeth Cleaning?

Professional teeth cleaning helps remove plaque, tartar, and stains that regular brushing and flossing can’t reach. This preventive treatment keeps your gums healthy, protects against cavities, and leaves your smile feeling fresh and bright. Regular cleanings are an essential part of maintaining long-term oral health.
